Frequently Asked Questions

How often should commercial ducts be cleaned?

Most Atlanta facilities benefit from cleaning every 3–5 years. High-dust environments (warehouses, schools, manufacturing) often need more frequent service.

Can you work in occupied buildings?

Yes. We use containment, sectional cleaning, and flexible scheduling (nights/weekends) to keep disruption minimal.

What documentation is provided?

Full before/after report with photos, system assessment, and cleaning certification for your records.
